Hidden gems

Big Ben, the Tower of London, and Trafalgar Square are renowned landmarks in London that you must visit, but I'd encourage you to explore beyond these famous attractions. You'll be pleasantly surprised by the abundance of hidden treasures that lie off the beaten tourist path. London has an array of lesser-known historical sights, trendy shopping spots, and various other delightful places waiting to be discovered. If you're looking for some unique experiences on your next visit, here are some of them worth seeking out.
